Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I get started with the app?
Download abc from the App Store, create an account, and you're ready to start tracking your personal finances. The onboarding guide will walk you through the key features.
Q: How do I add a transaction?
Tap the "+" button on the home screen, enter the transaction details (amount, category, date), and save. You can also edit or delete transactions at any time.
Q: How do I export my data?
Go to Settings, select "Export Data," and choose your preferred format (CSV or PDF). The exported file can be shared via email or saved to Files.
Q: My data is not syncing. What should I do?
Make sure you have an active internet connection. Try pulling down to refresh, or restart the app. If the issue persists, contact us at frghr@gmt.com.
Q: Is my financial data secure?
Yes. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We do not share your personal financial information with third parties. For more details, please review our Privacy Policy.

Note: Account deletion is irreversible. All your transaction history, settings, and personal data will be permanently removed.
